Binoculars v.1.0

This low poly 3d model (1364 tris) can be used as a FPP/TPP melee weapon.
The package comes with 1 material with 512x512 base texture (specular on alpha channel) 
+ 512x512 normal map in uncompressed TGA format and model in FBX format. 
Ready to use prefabs - drag and drop to your scene. 
A demo scene is also included.
